The eDUcore MOO database is not available at this time for public release.  
The licenses under which it will be released may be found in this 
directory <http://www.du.org/educore/database/>. They are:

eDUcore_noncommercial.txt
	License for noncommercial use (including individual components).
eDUcore_commercial.txt
	License for commercial use of the complete eDUcore.
eDUcore_commobject.txt
	License for commercial use of individual eDUcore components.

In addition, the eDUcore's online help system texts are available as complete 
documents in the Manuals area <http://www.du.org/educore/database/manuals/>.
